About

Hi, I'm Zach. I created Axe Charts to provide IATF Premier axe throwers with a more comprehensive view of their data than what Axe Scores currently offers. Additionally, I added a few features that I thought the community might like:

  • Axe Charts Rating (ACR) is an alternative to the Collins Rating System (CRS)
  • Badges are a way of providing some fun performance milestones to earn
