About

Axe Charts was created to provide IATF Premier axe throwers with a more comprehensive view of their performance than what Axe Scores currently offers. Building on top of that, I've added a few features to address some of the requests from the community.

Badges

"It would be nice to have other milestones to hit besides the 81."

Badges provide a fun list of performance milestones to aim for while chasing down that 81.

Rating System

"I hate that I can win a match and lose CRS points."

The Axe Charts Rating (ACR) is an alternative to CRS which should address this concern.
