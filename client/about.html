About

Hi, I'm Zach. I created Axe Charts to provide IATF Premier axe throwers with a more comprehensive view of their data than what Axe Scores currently offers. Additionally, I added a few features that I thought the community might like. Axe Charts Rating (ACR) is an alternative to the Collins Rating System (CRS) and Badges are a way of providing some additional performance milestones apart from whether or not you've hit an 81.
