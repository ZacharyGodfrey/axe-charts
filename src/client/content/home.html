Why another stats website?

Axe Charts was created to address three key issues with AxeScores.com: 1) Premier stats are not given the same visibility as Standard stats, 2) the player comparison is broken, and 3) the current charts don't provide much detail on performance trends over time.

Note: All data on this site is pulled from the official IATF source on a weekly basis. Only the top 100 Premier players have profile pages right now. All other profiles will be added soon.
